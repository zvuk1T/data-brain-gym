# 🧠🏋️ Data Brain Gym

**A public training space for SQL, Python, problem solving, interview communication, and cognitive fitness in the age of AI.**

---

## Why this exists

AI is becoming a powerful assistant in coding, analysis, writing, and problem solving.

That is a good thing.

But I do not want to outsource all of my thinking to AI.

Just as machines reduced physical labor and made physical fitness more important, AI reduces some forms of cognitive labor and makes deliberate thinking practice more important.

**Data Brain Gym** is my personal training system for keeping my technical thinking sharp.

---

## What "Data" means

The name has a double meaning:

- **data** — analytics, SQL, Python, pipelines, reporting, and AI systems
- **Data** — my working nickname / avatar across my AI and engineering projects
- **Brain Gym** — a place for regular mental training

So this is both:

> a brain gym for data work

and

> Data's personal brain gym

## Core rule

> I often know more than I need to say.  
> The goal is to answer clearly, calmly, and briefly — not to explain everything at once.

This applies especially to interviews, SQL walkthroughs, Python explanations, and project presentations.

---

## How I train

```text
1. Solve a small problem
2. Explain the solution
3. Review what was hard
4. Repeat consistently
```

Typical practice areas:

- SQL queries
- Python / pandas analysis
- business metrics
- logic problems
- data math
- STARL behavioural answers
- project explanations in English and German
