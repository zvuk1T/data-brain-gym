## 📄 Files

### **recruiting-lens-framework.md** ⭐ START HERE
The portfolio strategy for your DataCamp coursework. Explains:
- **Five signals recruiters observe** in your work (discipline, error analysis, business relevance, wisdom extraction, interview readiness)
- **Three interview simulation scenarios** with structured responses
- **How to talk about your learning journey** when interviewing

**For whom:** Anyone preparing for technical interviews or wanting to understand how your coursework signals competence.

**Key insight:** Your DataCamp work isn't just XP points. Every lesson encodes signals about how you think, learn, and apply knowledge. This framework helps you articulate that.

**For whom:** Curious about the *why* behind the structure.
